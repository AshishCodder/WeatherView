what is Temperature ?

what is Min Temperature ?

Minimum temperatures of an area or place is the lowest temperature at that place in a given time period.
The lowest possible temperature is -273 degree Celsius, or 0 Kelvin( Kelvin is a unit of temperature).Further More(Click Here)

what is Max Temperature ?

Max Temperature is the highest temperature at a place in a given time period .

what is Wind Speed ?

The measurement of moving air velocity. In meteorology, wind speed or wind flow speed, is a fundamental atmospheric quantity caused by air moving from high to low pressure, usually due to changes in temperature.Wind speed is now commonly measured with an Anemometer.Further More(Click Here)

what is Wind Degree/Direction ?

The direction FROM which the wind is blowing (degrees). Clockwise: north — 360 º (0 º), East — 90 º, South — 180 º, West — 270 º.Further More(Click Here)

what is Cloud Percentage ?

Cloud cover or cloud amount is total part of the sky covered by clouds expressed as a percentage.
For example, if the sky is 100% covered by clouds, it is a totally cloudy day. If the cloud cover is 25% — the day is more clear than cloudy. The cloud cover of 0% means it is a clear day with no visible clouds at all. So it is also called the percentage of the cloud coverage of the sky.

what is Humidity ?

Humidity is the concentration of water vapor present in the air. Water vapor, the gaseous state of water, is generally invisible to the human eye.
Humidity indicates the likelihood for precipitation, dew, or fog to be present.Further More(Click Here)
